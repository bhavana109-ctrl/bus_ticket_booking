Terms and Conditions and Cancellation Policy
Booking Terms
  • Passengers must arrive at the boarding point at least 30 minutes before departure.
  • A valid government-issued ID must be carried during travel and shown when requested.
  • Seats are confirmed only after successful payment.
  • Boarding and dropping points selected during booking must be followed for the trip.
  • Operator timings and stop schedules may change slightly because of traffic, weather, or operational reasons.
  • BusHub is a booking platform; onboard amenities may vary slightly based on operator availability.
Cancellation Policy
  • Cancellation is not allowed within 2 hours of departure.
  • 90% refund is issued for cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure.
  • 50% refund is issued for cancellations made between 2 and 24 hours before departure.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.
  • Convenience fees or operator deductions may be non-refundable where applicable.
Passenger Responsibilities
  • Please keep your phone, ticket, and contact details available during boarding.
  • Passengers are responsible for personal belongings.
  • Disruptive behavior, unsafe items, or violation of travel rules may result in denied boarding without refund.
